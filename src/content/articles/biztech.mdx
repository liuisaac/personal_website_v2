## Experience Overview 📋
<p>
UBC BizTech is the largest technology club at the University of British Columbia, dedicated to bridging the gap between business, technology, and students from all backgrounds. Through a wide range of events—from conferences and case competitions to tech panels and hackathons, BizTech's events provide valuable opportunities for students to engage, connect, and network with industry professionals.
