## Project Overview 📋

ChooChoo is a lightweight in-memory distributed key-value store built in Go, designed for simplicity and packaged in one executable. It leverages Raft for consensus in a single-leader replication model and BoltDB for WAL and snapshotting. It is primarily intended as a toy project to better understand the workings of similar distributed databases (ie: Apache Cassandra, Riak, Dynamo).
